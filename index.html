About Me

The person
behind the
pixels.

I'm Precious — a Lead Product Designer based in Lagos, Nigeria, building at the intersection of design, technology, and Africa's next chapter. I go by Buildwith_Pr3cioux because I do more than design — I ship.

Currently leading product design at Moovable, Africa's Logistics Operating System, where I work across product, brand, and visual design. I am also building my own products — including CRIB, a proptech platform helping Nigerian renters pay rent in flexible installments.

I believe the best products are the ones that feel inevitable — where the design gets out of the way and the experience just works. That's what I'm chasing with every project I take on.
